Branching Narrative

Many Choices, Many Outcomes

In Nether After, you will take on missions where your decisions change the story.

For example, in one scenario, you’ll be scavenging with your friend Aiden and come across someone caught in a trap, named Noah. You may want to help him, but Aiden insists on leaving with the loot you’ve found. Some choices will result in rescuing Noah, but allowing Aiden to die, and if this happens, other members of your group (Maya and Lukas) will blame you for his death and threaten to leave.
